OpenSim Freebies

22nd Dec, 2014 | Back

There's a post on Hypergrid Business where they want OpenSim users to vote for their favorite freebie destinations. This list is huge and I didn't even realize there were so many different places that had freebies, so I'm going to visit them all and tell you about them.

151.229.171.32:8002

This location did not come up on the map and I didn't find a Wifi page for them although I can ping the IP. I have a feeling Hypergrid Business didn't actually check any of these locations themselves and just pulled them from the previous survey they did.

192.99.101.85:8002:Ecueil Blanc

Also couldn't connect to this location, but I also couldn't ping the IP.

anettes-welt.de:8002


This brought me to a hypergate plaza with a handful of destinations, including one for freebies. It's the same Linda Kellie freebie mall we've seen everywhere else.

craft-world.org:8002:craft-store


This is one of my favorite freebie spots, it's even on my hypergrid teleport board. Lots of stuff for guys and girls including clothes, hair, and animation overriders.

craft-world.org:8002:monte gordo


I think this is the home of Virtual Christine and not really a freebie spot.

craft-world.org:8002:social plaza

This region does not exist.

craft-world.org:8002:spirit of arcadia

This region also doesn't exist. This list was most definitely not vetted before it was posted to the Hypergrid Business website.

craft-world.org:8002:the spirit of arcadia


This region does exist and is probably what was meant by the previous list item. Like Monte Gordo, this isn't really a freebie spot, but there are random items you can take a copy of and lots of blam gates to other locations.

dorenas-world.de:8002:actos


Several unique items here, including a few adult items. Avatars, clothes, some animation overriders, and a bunch of trees.

dorenas-world.de:8002:santiago


Lots of stuff here I haven't seen other places, including housewares, clothes, shoes, avatar parts, poseballs, and some mesh clothing.

fleepgrid.com:8002


Some original freebies for your educational type builds, clothes, scripts, and some Linda Kellie freebies here.

grid.3rdrockgrid.com:8002


There is a mall with many things for sale in the local currency, a second mall with many other things for sale in OS$, and a free avatars area with a few different offerings, including furries and child avatar (all appropriate).

grid.kitely.com:8002:Cookie II


Just a few freebies here, mostly just a neat place to explore.

grid.kitely.com:8002:Donnybrook Beach


There's a spot with a few of the same freebies you'll find most other places and a few things for sale in OS$.

grid.kitely.com:8002:Freebie Mall 2.0


Just the Linda Kellie freebie mall we all know and love.

grid.kitely.com:8002:French Quarter


A cute place, but I didn't find any freebies. A few stores with some things for sale in OS$.
