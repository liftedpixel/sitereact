I saw this jumper that says 'Kawaii in the streets, Sempai in the sheets' on Plurk and had to have it. For only $5L it was a pretty sweet deal, too. [ parfait. ], the store it's from, is one I had not been in before and it's been added to the list. The store has lots of kawaii stuff like frilly maid dresses and polo shirts with Pokemon designs. It's definitely worth checking out if you're into that kind of thing.


Since this is my first fashion blog post in a long time, let me tell you about my 'fashion sense'. I am not one to spend a lot of money in Second Life. I mostly pay for land and that's it. When I make an outfit, it's usually a bunch of old items I still like together with a few new items I have purchased. There are only a few modern pieces in this outfit, if you can't tell.


The M3 anime head by Utilizator is a relatively new purchase for me. I'm not really into mesh body parts, but I thought I'd give it a try because it looked really fun. I'm not an anime all the time, but it's a fun accessory. There is a weird line in the middle of the nose with most appliers, but some people might like it. The lipstick is a free applier I picked up from a store at Annie May Haven. If you like looking at creative, well put together avatars, consider sitting around AMH for a bit.
